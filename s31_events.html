ISLS Congress

Since 2017, we have been organizing the International Advanced Liver and Pancreas Symposium every year. The previous ISLS symposiums mainly focused on invited lectures; however, ISLS is now capable of hosting the congress with a fully diverse scientific program, including free paper presentation sessions. The official name of the regular meeting will be changed to "The ISLS Congress of Advanced HBP Surgery."

We have changed the annual symposium to a biennial congress and will organize small conferences in Seoul or Asia in the years when the congress is not held.

The 5th Congress of International Advanced HBP Surgery, the first of which was successfully held in Zurich, Switzerland in October 2023.

The 6th Congress of International Advanced HBP Surgery will be held in Canada in October 2025. Since it is a biennial congress, the congress after that will be held in 2027.
