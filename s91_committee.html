Introduction of the ISLS Committees

Under the Executive Council, five committees has been established to support the expansion and development of the society. Each committee contributes to advancing knowledge, supporting education, and fostering networking among ISLS members.
ISLS member may join the ISLS Committees with the approval of the ISLS Executive Council. The roles and current members of each committee are listed below.
